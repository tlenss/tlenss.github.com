PHP 5.3 Almost Ready for Release.

Jun 26th, 2009

The long awaited 5.3 version is near it’s final stage. I’ve been really looking forward to this.

1. July 24th - feature freeze COMPLETED
  1. July 31st - alpha1 COMPLETED
  2. September 2nd - alpha2 (freeze on August 29th, final packaging on September 1st) COMPLETED
  3. December 4th - alpha3 (freeze on December 2nd, final packaging on December 3rd) COMPLETED
  4. January 29th - beta1 (freeze on January 26th, final packaging on January 28th) COMPLETED
  5. March 24th - RC1 (bug fixing until the 19th, with RM ok commits until 20nd, until 23rd build fixes only) COMPLETED
  6. June 7th - RC2 (bug fixing until end of the 4th, after that only build fixes and README changes) COMPLETED
  7. June 11th - RC3 COMPLETED
  8. June 18th - RC4 COMPLETED
  9. June 30th - stable
  10. Continued RC releases in 2-4 week intervals
  11. Stable release around Q2 2009
